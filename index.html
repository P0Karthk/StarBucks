50 years of serving communities

Starbucks has even more ways to celebrate 50 years of serving communities and uplifting customers with the debut of the Starbucks Starland: 50th Anniversary Edition game. Starting Monday, October 4 and running through Sunday, October 24, Starbucks Rewards members can play the game for the chance to win more than 2 million prizes including free drinks for 50 days, 50 weeks or 50 months, plus a gift card that can be redeemed to purchase Starbucks stock or an all-inclusive trip to a Starbucks Reserve Roastery in Seattle, Chicago or New York.

Starbucks Rewards members can earn a game “play” by making an eligible purchase at a participating Starbucks location in the U.S. Limit is two plays per day. The purchase must be made using a Starbucks Card, a linked payment method, or by scanning the Starbucks app prior to checkout.
